[NTU 108] Meta analysis

林先和 杜裕康 張齡尹

2020-01-12

Description

This course will introduce students to the principles and key statistical concepts in conducting a meta-analysis quantitative review of randomised controlled trials and observational studies. The course will be structured by the critical steps in conducting a meta-analysis, with particular emphasis on common pitfalls when doing such an analysis. The course consists of lectures and computer labs using R language for meta-analysis.

Objective

The goal of the course is to provide the students the general framework of conducting a meta-analysis. At the end of the course the students are expected to be able to read and critically appraise the literature of meta-analysis and to conduct basic meta-analysis.
